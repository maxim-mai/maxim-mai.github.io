A bit about me

I am a Heisenberg Fellow at the Albert Einstein Center at the University of Bern. Additionally to this, I am an adjunct Research Professor at The George Washington University and a Privatdozent at the University of Bonn.

My main interests include the nature of excited states of matter, non-perturbative Quantum Chromodynamics, Lattice QCD, Effective Field Theories, Quantization Conditions, Machine/model selection applications. For more details click on the images below or go to the research section.
