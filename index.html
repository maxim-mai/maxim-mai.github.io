Research

My research explores the strong interaction - one of the four fundamental forces of nature. Quantum field theory (accounting for special relativity and quantum effects) of this interaction is called Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD). In the low energy regime QCD defines the mechanisms for the interaction of hadrons (protons, ...) as well as their properties. In particular, the vast part of the mass of hadrons and thus of the most part of Universe is due to QCD.
