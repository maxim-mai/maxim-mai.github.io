About Me


I am a theoretical physicist in nuclear and hadron physics. My main interest include origin of excited states of matter, Quantum Chromodynamics in continuum and on the lattice, Effective Field Theories, Quantization conditions, Machine learning and model selection applications. Currently, I am working as a Senior Fellow at the Helmholtz-Institut fuer Strahlen- und Kernphysik in Bonn. Before, I was an assistant research professor (faculty title) at The George Washington University. Feel free to contact me for any questions to my research or for teaching materials.
